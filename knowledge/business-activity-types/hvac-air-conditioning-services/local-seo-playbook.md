# Local SEO Playbook — HVAC

## Search Intent Model
HVAC search is mostly transactional and local. Pages should combine service, location, equipment type, and problem intent.

## Core Page Architecture
- City hub: صيانة مكيفات بالرياض
- Service pages: تركيب، تنظيف، إصلاح، فك وتركيب، تعبئة فريون
- Equipment pages: سبليت، شباك، مركزي، دكت، كاسيت
- Audience pages: منازل، فلل، شركات، مطاعم، مكاتب
- District pages: neighborhood-specific pages where service coverage is real
- Problem pages: المكيف لا يبرد، تسريب ماء، ضعف التبريد، صوت مزعج

## Google Business Profile
Primary category recommendation:
- Air Conditioning Contractor

Additional category candidates:
- HVAC Contractor
- Air Conditioning Repair Service
- Appliance Repair Service
- Commercial Refrigeration, only when genuinely offered

## On-Page Requirements
Each service page should include:
- clear service promise
- city/service-area mention
- equipment types covered
- common problems solved
- response and booking method
- warranty or guarantee if available
- FAQ section
- internal links to related services
- conversion CTA for phone and WhatsApp

## Trust Signals
- customer reviews
- before/after photos
- technician experience
- warranty terms
- service area clarity
- transparent inspection process
- commercial maintenance contract examples
