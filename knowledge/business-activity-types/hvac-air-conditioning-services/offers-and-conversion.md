# Offers and Conversion Model — HVAC

## Conversion Actions
- WhatsApp message
- phone call
- booking form
- maintenance contract inquiry
- inspection request

## High-Converting Offers
- seasonal AC maintenance before summer
- split AC cleaning package
- central AC inspection
- company maintenance contract
- restaurant cooling inspection
- multi-unit villa maintenance package
- installation with workmanship guarantee

## Conversion Page Pattern
1. Problem or service headline
2. Local relevance
3. Service scope
4. Equipment types
5. Proof and trust
6. Warranty or quality promise
7. Simple booking CTA
8. FAQs addressing price, timing, warranty, and service coverage

## Lead Qualification Fields
- customer type: residential or commercial
- city / district
- AC type
- service needed
- number of units
- urgency
- preferred contact method
