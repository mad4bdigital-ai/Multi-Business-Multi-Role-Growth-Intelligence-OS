## Definition
A local service business that installs, maintains, repairs, cleans, relocates, and optimizes air conditioning and cooling systems for residential and commercial customers.

## Core Market Logic
This activity is driven by urgent demand, seasonal demand, and planned installation demand. Customers usually search with high commercial intent and a local modifier such as city, district, or near-me intent.

## Primary Customer Segments
- Residential: apartments, villas, homes, tenants, property owners
- Commercial: offices, restaurants, cafes, shops, clinics, schools, small and medium businesses
- Facility and property managers needing recurring maintenance
